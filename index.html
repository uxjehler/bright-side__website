Find the best treatment for you

 

Complete a 10-minute online questionnaire and our system will help you evaluate the depression treatment options most likely to benefit you, including antidepressant medication, therapy, and lifestyle changes.

A US licensed physician will review your information, verify the best treatment, and send your prescription to your local pharmacy if antidepressant medication is right for you. Only pay a $20 visit fee ($40 without insurance).

Good Measure’s mission is to help people lead healthy, happy, and productive lives by improving access to the highest quality depression care.

Access anywhere

 

Stigma, cost, and availability limit people’s ability to get the depression treatment they need. Telemedicine can make it easy for anyone to get high quality and affordable care, when and where it works for them.

Better prescribing with data

 

Only 20% of depressed people receive treatment consistent with practice guidelines. Our proprietary knowledge base uses data to help physicians match patients to the treatment most likely to help them.

A measured approach

 

Measurement Based Care involves measuring symptoms over time with treatment. We proactively use this data to ensure each patient is progressing and modify treatment as necessary, helping more people get better, faster.
